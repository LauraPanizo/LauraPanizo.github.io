About me


I am Telecommunication engineer and PhD in Computer Science from the University of Málaga. I received my PhD in 2013 and worked as postdoctoral researcher at MORSE research group, which is part of the Institute of Technology and Software Engineering (ITIS). From 2018 until 2021, I have worked as interim Assistant Professor in the Computer Science department at the University of Málaga. In 2021, I promoted to Assistant Professor in the same department.  
My research interests include formal methods for Cyber-Physical Systems (model checking, model-based testing, runtime verification, etc.), focusing on embedded and mobile communication systems. I have participated in different European projects such as the SAID project, designing and developing a decision support tool for dam management, the TRIANGLE project, in the automatic generation of mobile apps models and the use of model-based testing techniques, or the EuWireless project, in the design of a pan-European research infrastructure for mobile communication.
